A letter from my heart...

For Nikita

Some feelings are too heavy for a message. So I built this little place to say what I never could.

Dear Nikita,

I don't know if you'll read every word. I don't know how you'll feel. But I wanted my apology to be more than just another text.

There were moments where I should have listened. Moments where I should have understood you better. Instead... I reacted.

Looking back, I realise something. Love was never about proving who was right. It was always about protecting what we had.

I'm sorry for every tear my actions caused.

Thank you for every smile, every hug, every memory, every moment that made my life beautiful.

I'm not writing this to pressure you. I'm writing this because you deserved a sincere apology.

Whether our paths meet again or not... I'll always be grateful that they crossed.

With love,

Saurabh ❤️

For Nikita

This isn't a website. It's everything I couldn't say.

Dear Nikita,

Some stories are unforgettable. Ours is one of them.

Maybe life changed. Maybe emotions changed. Maybe time changed. But one thing never changed... The place you have in my heart.

I know I made mistakes. I know I hurt you. I know there were moments when I should have listened instead of reacting.

I wasn't trying to win. I was trying to save what I believed was our love.

If I could relive those moments, I would choose patience over ego. Understanding over assumptions. Love over anger. Every single time.

Thank you for every smile, every hug, every late-night conversation, every memory. You made my world beautiful.

I'm not asking you to forget everything. I'm not asking you to come back. I only wanted you to know that my apology is genuine.

You deserved a better version of me. And I'm becoming that person even if life never brings us together again.

I'm Sorry ❤️

— Saurabh
